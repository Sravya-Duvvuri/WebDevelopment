Help Center


Home

Welcome to the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Alumni Website! The Home tab provides you with an overview of the latest news and events related to our alumni community.


Search

The Search tab allows you to search for specific alumni profiles and connect with your fellow alumni. You can search by name, batch, department, or other criteria.


View Profile

The View Profile tab is where you can view and edit your own profile information. You can update details like your job, higher education, and description.


Alumni Meets

Alumni Meets are annual events where you can reunite with old friends, make new connections, and stay updated on the latest happenings at our institution. Each meet is a great opportunity to network and celebrate your alma mater. You can also view all the past Alumni Meets you have RSVP'd to and any content you might have saved in your journal page. You can view this in your View Profile Page as well.


Chat

The Chat tab provides you with a platform to connect and chat with other alumni. Share your experiences, exchange ideas, and build a stronger alumni community.


Help

If you have any questions or need assistance, the Help tab is here for you. You can find answers to frequently asked questions or contact our support team for further assistance.



Contact Us

In case you need help regarding anything or want to request a change in details, then please contact us at the email ID: help.amrita.cb@cb.amrita.edu
